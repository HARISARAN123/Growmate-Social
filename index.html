Our Story

Growmate was created by a group of students passionate about solving the challenges farmers face with soil and plant health. We developed tools that provide instant support and promote learning in agriculture. By sharing knowledge and earning rewards, farmers can enhance their skills and make informed decisions. Together, we envision a thriving agricultural community dedicated to sustainability.
